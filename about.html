Intro: About Ryan - SEO Specialist from the Philippines

Hi! My name is Bert Ryan Rodriguez holding a Baachelor's degree in Computer Science from Visayas State University. I started out writing code in JavaScript, PHP, C++, HTML, and CSS—and even got pretty handy with Linux along the way.

Tech has always been my comfort zone, but over time I realized I wanted to do more than just build things. I wanted to help people and businesses get found online. That curiosity pushed me into SEO, where I now bring together my technical background and a knack for problem-solving to make websites visible, optimized, and impactful.

These days I live in Quezon City with my wife and daughter, who are my biggest motivation to keep growing. Transitioning into SEO hasn’t just been a career move—it’s been an exciting way to connect my skills with real-world results.
